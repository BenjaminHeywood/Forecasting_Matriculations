Forecasting Matriculations

This dashboard presents forecasted matriculations generated by a Gradient Boosted Decision Tree model trained on historical applicant snapshot data. The forecasts shown are based on the most recent admissions snapshot for Semester 1, 2026/27 and estimate the number of current applicants expected to matriculate. Use the filters to view forecasts by Controlled Status and Fee Status. Forecasts are summarised by Faculty, Level of Study (UG, RPG, TPG) and Region to support recruitment monitoring and planning. Values represent expected matriculations and may include decimal values because they are aggregated model predictions rather than counts of individual applicants.
